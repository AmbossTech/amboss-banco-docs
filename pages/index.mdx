### How does MiBanco ensure the security of my assets?

MiBanco employs advanced hashing and encryption methods, including ChaCha20, Argon2id, and HMAC-SHA256, to protect your assets and sensitive information with robust end-to-end encryption.

### What cryptocurrencies does MiBanco support?

MiBanco currently supports Bitcoin and Tether.

### Can I use MiBanco for everyday transactions?

Absolutely! MiBanco is designed to facilitate both everyday transactions and more advanced cryptocurrency activities, making it versatile and user-friendly.

### Is there a limit to how much cryptocurrency I can store on MiBanco?

MiBanco does not impose specific limits on the amount of cryptocurrency you can store, but ensure you adhere to best practices for security and storage.

### How do I create a new wallet with MiBanco?

During the account creation process, MiBanco creates an initial wallet. Later you can create more wallets using the Banco client.

### How does MiBanco interact with the Lightning Network?

MiBanco uses swaps provided by [Boltz](https://boltz.exchange/) to send and receive over the Lightning Network.

### Can I change my lightning address?

It is not currently possible to change your Lightning address.

### How does the Lightning address work?

The Lightning address is using swaps behind the scenes to allow you to receive and send Lightning payments without needing to run a Lightning node.

### What happens if I lose my mnemonic or password?

Your mnemonic is encrypted and stored on Banco servers which you can decrypt using your account password. If you forget your password, your sensitive information is unrecoverable.

### What is the role of the symmetric key in MiBanco?

The symmetric key is used for encrypting and decrypting your sensitive data, ensuring that only you can access your private information.
